There is no elevator in being the expert on bitcoin/blockchain you have to take the stairs. The technology is very complex - but incredibly powerful. I would suggest you absorb as much information as you can - podcasts, Youtube videos (links at the bottom of this post).

It also depends on why you want to be an expert? Are you technical? Are you approaching it from a business perspective? or as an entrepreneur?

If you are a business person and want to know enough to give you a rounded approach to the technology and ton understand how the technology is being approached from a PRACTICAL perspective then try my book that has just been released on Kindle. It is from a business perspective and written as a business person for business people. I have a blockchain business that we are getting off the ground but so many organsiations we have spoken to kept asking what is the blockchain all about - so I wrote Down The Rabbit Hole to answer those questions.

Amazon.com: Blockchain: Down The Rabbit Hole: (Discover The Power Of The Blockchain) eBook: Tim Lea: Kindle Store

If you want more technical details - try the works By Andreas Antenopolous - his books are more technical. I am slightly biased but my book is a great primer for all you need to know to understand the core potential and power of the blockchain and will give you more of a foundation before you check out the heavy duty technical podcasts below. Either way just absorb as much as you can - the technology is moving soooo quickly it is sometimes very hard to stay in touch - so being an expert on everything is increasingly hard - and you will get out only what you put in.

Good luck !!
